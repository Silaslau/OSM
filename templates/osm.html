Now shift your attention to the center of the scene, spanning from left 23 to right 53 degrees. Right there is the stunning Shaw Auditorium, a masterpiece of contemporary architecture. It feels quite close—just 20 to 22 meters away—inviting and accessible as a center for performance, gathering, and cultural exchange.
